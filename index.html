Bi-weekly
Coding
Challenges

Every two weeks, we drop a fresh coding challenge. Spend 10–20 minutes sharpening your skills, solving real problems, and competing with friends on the leaderboard. Whether you're a first-year writing your first function or a final-year prepping for interviews, these challenges are your bi-weekly dose of fun, practice, and community. Earn points, win bragging rights, and become a better developer, one challenge at a time.
